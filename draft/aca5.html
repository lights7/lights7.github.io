为了在美华人的未来，请反对ACA5

普林斯顿的社会学家做过研究，在SAT（美国大学入学考试）里一个亚裔要比白人小孩高出140分，比拉丁裔高出270分，比非裔学生高出450分，才能有同等入学机会。

严重伤害华人的ACA5法案已经在加州众议院通过，下一步要在参议院投票，而我们要做的就是给参议院的议员们写邮件来提出反对意见。

代表圣地牙哥的非洲裔加州女众议员韦博，正式提出ACA 5法案，要求全面取消加州在1996年通过的209提案。

209 法案源于1996年，该法案禁止加州政府（包括加州公立大学）在教育，雇佣，和合同中使用种族，性别，肤色，民族或原籍等因素歧视或者优待任何个人或者群体。

这项法案的执行机构包括州、县、市政府，公立大学系统，社区学院，学区，或任何其他机构。

而该法案最大争议之一是大学录取。这反映在公立大学招生上，禁止大学在录取中使用种族偏好的规定。简单一个词：付出努力，达到标准你就上,不努力学习，没有达到录取标准就不能上。学校不能因为你什么族裔就不让你上。

所以我们一直以来看到华裔孩子刻苦读书，积极参加各类社区公益活动，他们在学业上和其他方面的优异表现，让他们在各族裔孩子中表现突出，获得了更多的高校录取率。

但同时拉丁裔和非裔的录取率就下来了。

ACA5现在就是要废除以上的法律，让学校可以种族偏好却不算违法，而ACA 5
给亚裔入学配比只有12%。原本亚裔在UC大学的入学比例是42%，这是考进去的。

众所周知亚裔努力学习的多，现在这个法案要强制执行种族比例，把亚裔入学改为12%， 剩下的位置让给非裔和西裔。

ACA5已经在众议院通过，参议院Appropriations Committee听证会确定于6/23周二9am开始 , 递交反对意见的时间已经过了。

下一步是最关键的参议院在6月底的投票，还有几天的时间

希望大家能利用这最后的几天给每个参议院的议员发反对信

邮件一个一个地发，一对一显得真诚些。

无论成数如何，我们都要尽力而为，现锁定9个民主党参议员，亦请民众发信给他们及11位共和党参议员，若有此11票再加民主党3票便可成功挡下ACA5恶法。

给民主党议员的Email Sample Letter:

Subject: Strong opposition to ACA5 which is a discriminatory and unacceptable amendment

从下面开始复制，只需复制一次。

California Assembly Constitutional Amendment No. 5 aims to repeal Prop 209 approved by voters in 1996. If approved, it will give rise to the use of race, national origin, and gender as evaluation basis in the public sector of California for college admission, job application, and contract approval. Please definitely reject ACA5. We cannot support you if you approve this discriminatory and regressive amendment!

Everybody should be judged objectively based on merits but not on immutable traits such as color of the skin/national origin or gender. Otherwise it is discrimination based on race or sex, in accordance with the current federal and state law! A March 2019 Pew study with about 6,600 respondents finds that the majority of Americans, 73%, believe that colleges and universities should not consider race or ethnicity in admissions. In this survey, 65% of Hispanic Americans, 62% of African Americans, and 63% of Democrats reject considering the race factor for college admissions. This would likely be the result if ACA5 was on the ballot.

As Martin Luther King said, “I look to a day when people will not be judged by the color of their skin, but by the content of their character.”ACA5 will prevent Dr. King’s dream from becoming true.

Diversity is a major excuse of the proponents of ACA5 which will actually cause more ill feelings among races. Diversity should not supersede equality and justice. Evaluating an applicant based on race or gender is discrimination, exactly contradictory to equality and is unjust. U.C. already has methods to give preference to students with poorer family background.

Please do not be misled. ACA5 creates more baneful discrimination problems for different races, rather than solving any. It will eventually even lower the level of excellence of different aspects in California especially in the academic area.

When there are astoundingly tens of thousands of letters to California legislators against ACA5, why no Democratic legislators dared to reject this outrageous amendment so far?! Please vote according to your conscience and proper principles of equality, justice, and democracy, but not according to party line! Please show us that we have genuine democracy in California, vote NO on ACA5! Thank you.

Sincerely,
